RealTune - Version 1.0
======================

Improved radio tuning. Use your mouse-wheel to tune radios quickly and efficiently 
in one place instead of digging through radio stack panel(s) and finding those
gauge image-hotspots.
 
Author  : Nils Meier
email   : nils.meier@gmail.com
homepage: http://code.google.com/p/fscode/wiki/RealTune

Installation:
------------

 Copy RealTune.dll into the module-directory of your local Flight 
 Simulator installation - normally:

  c:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Flight Simulator 9\Modules

 Enjoy!

Uninstall:
----------

 Delete RealTune.dll from your module-directory of your local Flight
 Simulator installation (see Installation).
